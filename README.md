## About me

Hello! I am a Software Engineer with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and professional experience working on production-level software used by multiple clients.

My technical background is centered around **C#** and the **.NET ecosystem**, with experience developing **REST APIs**, working with different data storage technologies, and building full-stack solutions using **Angular**. I am also comfortable working in Agile environments, particularly **SCRUM-based workflows**, having participated in several projects following these methods. This environment strengthened my ability to collaborate effectively, adapt quickly, and deliver within iterative development cycles.

Alongside my professional work, I have participated in academic and organizational projects, including research-focused initiatives and leadership and event organization roles. These experiences strengthened my ability to communicate and lead, collaborate with diverse teams, and approach problems from multiple perspectives.

I am currently pursuing further specialization with a **masters degree** in Computer Engineering with a focus on **Game Development and Interactive Systems**. My interest in this area goes beyond technical implementation, driven by a desire to create meaningful, engaging experiences and to explore the creative and design aspects of games. While my long-term interests include game-related technologies, my primary professional focus is on growing as a software engineer working on complex systems and impactful products.
